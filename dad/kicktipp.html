The method, honestly
Dad’s ratings start at the FIFA points of June 11, 2026 (the final pre-tournament release) and are updated after every result (Elo, K=50, strictly walk-forward: dad never peeks at the future, he only reads yesterday’s scores over breakfast). The rating gap gives a win probability; scorelines follow two Poissons splitting a 2.7-goal budget; knockout draws run into extra time and penalties, where our Kicktipp counts every goal, so a 1-1 decided 4-3 from the spot is booked as 5:4 and dad never tips a knockout draw. He knows the expected Kicktipp points of every scoreline under our 2-4 rule and usually submits the best one — unless he likes a flag, in which case the scoreline drifts: tip t with probability ∝ exp(5·EP(t)). Each of the 10,000 dads below tips every played match that way and is scored against the verified results. Bonus questions are excluded on both sides. Dad is fictional. The mustache is not.
