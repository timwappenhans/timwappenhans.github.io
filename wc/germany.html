Predictions Results Spielbaum Germany
Group E won, into the round of 32. Germany closed the group stage with a much-changed side and lost 2-1 to Ecuador in East Rutherford, but still finished top of Group E on goal difference ahead of Ivory Coast, who also took six points. Leroy Sané struck inside two minutes, only for Nilson Angulo to level on nine minutes and Gonzalo Plata to win it for Ecuador on 77 against a heavily rotated lineup. With the knockout place already secured, Nagelsmann rested several starters, so the defeat was a harmless one: Germany top the group on six points and a plus-six difference. They now face one of the best third-placed teams in the round of 32 in Boston on Monday June 29. Felix Nmecha, who limped off injured against Ivory Coast, is still being assessed, while Serge Gnabry (adductor tear) and Lennart Karl (thigh) remain the long-term absences.
